About Me

I’m a creative graphic designer dedicated to turning concepts into wearable art and digital assets. I focus on precision, color theory, and creating visual identities that teams and brands are proud to wear.

Background

I am a visual designer with a focus on brand identity and apparel. I spent years honing my skills in Adobe Creative Suite, specifically in vector art and typography. My background is built on the belief that design should be functional as well as beautiful—bridging the gap between a digital concept and a tangible product that people are proud to wear.
